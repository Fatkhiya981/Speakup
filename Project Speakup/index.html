Our Story

Speakup was established on September 2021 and is one of online platform education which has the concept to help and train the younger generations to speak their thoughts and feelings on a matter through public speaking. Speakup helps younger generations to build their courage and guide them on how to use their voice through online classes that are provided in Speakup. Hence, Speakup can help improve young generation to improve their public speaking and communication skills which are necessary in the prospect of their education and career.

Our Aim

1. Provide a fun, educative, and safe education platform that focuses mainly to improve public speaking skill.

2. Develop competent and professional individuals by improving their public speaking skills through our classes and programs.

3. Committed to modeling and fostering diversity, equity, and inclusion for all people and communities with different background.

4. Connects, supports, and inspires a diverse community committed to empowering people through our platform.
